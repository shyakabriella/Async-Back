# AsyncAfrica Training Information

## Overview
AsyncAfrica offers practical technology training designed to help learners build real skills for work, projects, and career growth. Our training is focused on hands-on learning, guided practice, and useful knowledge that students can apply in real situations.

## Training Areas
We currently offer training in the following areas:

- Software Development
- Network and Internet Technology

## Internship Training Program
AsyncAfrica also offers an internship training program for learners who want practical experience and skill development.

### Internship Details
- **Duration:** 4 weeks
- **Training Fee:** 30,000 RWF
- **Outcome:** Participants gain practical skills and receive a certificate after successful completion

## What Learners Get
Learners who join the training or internship program can benefit from:

- Practical hands-on learning
- Real-world technology skills
- Guided support during training
- Experience in important technical areas
- A certificate after successful completion

## Payment Information
The accepted payment method is:

- **MTN Mobile Money**

## Registration and Payment Rules
To confirm registration, payment must be completed on time.

- A learner must register first
- After registration, payment must be made within **2 days**
- If payment is not completed within 2 days, the payment request becomes **invalid**
- If the payment becomes invalid, the learner must **register again**

## Important Notes
- Registration is only considered complete after payment is successfully made
- Learners are encouraged to pay as soon as possible after registration
- Delayed payment may lead to cancellation of the registration
- A new registration may be required if the payment window has expired
- Certificate issuance is based on successful participation and completion of the training or internship
